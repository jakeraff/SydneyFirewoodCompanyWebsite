About Us

At Sydney Firewood Company, we are dedicated to providing premium, sustainably sourced firewood that fuels warmth and comfort. For over 25 years, we have been the trusted supplier of premium firewood to Sydney's North, delivering warmth and reliability to homes and businesses alike.

What started as a small, local operation has grown into a respected name known for quality, sustainability, and exceptional service. We strive for convenient delivery and the perfect wood for every need.

We acquire our firewood from naturally fallen trees, offering premium ironbark and yellow box firewood ideal for burning. Our wood produces high heat with little smoke, perfect for both heating and cooking.

Sydney Firewood Company serves the Hills, North Shore & surrounding areas, bringing all your firewood products and accessories directly to your doorstep.
